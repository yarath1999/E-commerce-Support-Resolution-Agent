FIRST‑PARTY STORE POLICIES (GLOBAL) — SYNTHETIC SAMPLE
Effective date: 2026-03-30
Applies to: Purchases made directly from the brand’s first‑party storefront (web + mobile app) unless a product page or confirmation email states otherwise.

1) SCOPE & DEFINITIONS
“First‑party” means the item was sold and fulfilled by the brand (our warehouse or contracted carrier) and the payment was processed by us.
“Final sale” means the item is not eligible for return, exchange, or price adjustment except where required by law or where we made an error.
“Hygiene items” include (examples): cosmetics, skincare, underwear, swimwear, grooming tools, earphones/earbuds, and any item that touches the body or is sealed for sanitary reasons.
“Perishables” include (examples): food, beverages, supplements with temperature requirements, flowers, and other time‑sensitive or temperature‑sensitive goods.
“Opened items” means any product removed from original sealed packaging, or any sealed item whose tamper‑evident seal is broken.

2) RETURNS & REFUNDS (GENERAL RULES)
2.1 Return window
- Standard return window: 30 days from delivery date.
- Gift returns: 30 days from delivery date; refund is issued as store credit unless the original payer initiates a refund.
Condition: The return must be initiated within the window and the package must be handed to the carrier within 7 days of receiving a return authorization.

2.2 Item condition requirements
Eligible returns must meet all of the following unless explicitly waived:
- Item is unused, unwashed, and in resalable condition.
- Original packaging, manuals, and accessories are included.
- Any serial numbers/labels are intact and match the order.
If an item is returned used, incomplete, or materially altered, we may (a) decline the return, (b) deduct a restocking fee, or (c) return the item back to you at your cost.

2.3 Final sale items
- Items labeled “Final Sale” are not returnable or exchangeable.
Exception conditions:
- Item arrived damaged or incorrect.
- Item is defective within the applicable warranty period.
- Mandatory local consumer rights apply (see Regional Addendum).

2.4 Hygiene items
Hygiene items are only returnable if ALL conditions below are met:
- The item is unopened and the tamper seal is intact.
- The return is initiated within 14 days of delivery.
- The product is not listed as “non‑returnable” on the product page.
If a hygiene item is opened, we may deny the return for health and safety reasons, even if it is within the normal 30‑day window.

2.5 Perishables
Perishables are not eligible for return.
If there is an issue, we resolve it via replacement or refund under the Disputes section if:
- You report the problem within 48 hours of delivery, and
- You provide photos showing the condition at delivery.
We may require temperature/handling evidence where relevant.

2.6 Opened items (non‑hygiene)
For non‑hygiene items, opening the packaging does not automatically make the item non‑returnable.
However, if the item shows signs of use beyond reasonable inspection, we may apply a deduction or deny the return.
Examples of “reasonable inspection”: trying on shoes indoors on a clean surface, checking size/fit while keeping tags attached.

2.7 Refund method & timing
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method after the return is received and inspected.
- Processing time: typically 5–10 business days after inspection.
Conditions that can extend processing time:
- High return volume, incomplete packages, or mismatched serial numbers.
- Returns routed through international customs.
Shipping fees are non‑refundable unless the return is due to our error or a verified carrier failure.

2.8 Exchanges
Where available, exchanges are offered for the same item in a different size/color.
Conditions:
- The requested variant must be in stock.
- The returned item must meet condition requirements.
If exchange inventory is unavailable, we issue a refund instead.

3) CANCELLATIONS
3.1 Cancellation window
You may cancel an order within 60 minutes of placing it, unless the order has already entered fulfillment.
Condition: Orders that are “Ready to Ship,” “Shipped,” or “Handed to Carrier” cannot be cancelled.

3.2 Address changes
Address changes are treated as cancellations + reorders.
We can attempt a change only if:
- The request is made within the 60‑minute window, and
- The carrier label has not been created.
If the label is created, you may need to contact the carrier directly or wait for delivery and then initiate a return.

3.3 Pre‑orders and backorders
Pre‑orders may be cancellable until the item is allocated to a shipment.
Backorders may be cancelled any time before shipment, unless noted otherwise.
If a promotional discount applied, cancellation and reorder may cause the promotion to be re‑evaluated under current terms.

4) SHIPPING & DELIVERY
4.1 Standard handling and transit
Handling time is typically 1–2 business days. Transit times vary by destination and carrier.
Estimated delivery dates are estimates, not guarantees, unless an express guarantee is explicitly purchased.

4.2 Delays
We are not responsible for delays caused by:
- Weather, natural disasters, strikes, customs inspections, or carrier capacity.
- Incorrect address provided by the customer.
- Security screening or delivery restrictions.
If a package is delayed beyond 7 days past the latest estimate, we may offer one of the following at our discretion:
- A goodwill credit, or
- A reshipment after confirming the package is lost (see 4.3).

4.3 Lost package policy
A package is considered “lost” when:
- Tracking shows no movement for 7 consecutive days after shipment, OR
- Tracking shows “Delivered” but you report non‑receipt within 48 hours and delivery evidence is inconclusive.
Required conditions:
- You must confirm the shipping address and check safe locations/building mailroom.
- For higher‑value orders, we may require a signed statement or police report number, where legally allowed.
Resolution options:
- Reshipment (subject to inventory), or
- Refund to the original payment method.
If carrier records show delivery to the correct address with proof (e.g., GPS/photo) we may deny a refund, but we will still help you file a carrier claim.

5) PROMOTIONS & COUPON RULES
5.1 One coupon per order
Unless stated otherwise, only one coupon code can be applied per order.

5.2 Eligibility restrictions
Coupons may exclude:
- Final sale items
- Gift cards
- Perishables
- Bundles or subscription items
Conditions are shown in the promotion terms and may change by campaign.

5.3 Minimum spend and returns
If you return items from an order that used a minimum‑spend coupon and the post‑return subtotal falls below the threshold:
- The discount may be removed, and
- Your refund amount will be adjusted accordingly.

5.4 Misuse and fraud
We may cancel orders or disable coupons for misuse, including:
- Publicly posted “private” codes, automated scraping, or multiple account abuse.
- Resale behavior that violates our terms.
We will notify you if an order is cancelled for suspected abuse.

6) DISPUTES & ISSUE RESOLUTION
6.1 Damaged item
Report within 7 days of delivery.
Conditions:
- Provide photos of the item and outer packaging.
- Keep all packaging until the claim is resolved.
Resolution:
- Replacement or refund; for certain items we may request return shipment before replacement.

6.2 Incorrect item
Report within 14 days of delivery.
Conditions:
- Provide a photo of the item received and the packing slip.
Resolution:
- We ship the correct item (subject to stock) and provide a prepaid return label for the incorrect item, or
- We issue a refund if replacement is unavailable.

6.3 Chargebacks
Before initiating a chargeback, contact support so we can attempt a resolution.
If you file a chargeback while a return is in progress, the return may be paused until the dispute is resolved.

7) MARKETPLACE VS FIRST‑PARTY NOTE
If your order confirmation indicates the item was sold by a third‑party marketplace seller, different rules may apply (see Marketplace Addendum). In case of conflict, the seller policy and the platform’s mandatory rules govern.

CONTACT & RECORDKEEPING
Keep your order number and delivery address available when contacting support. We may request additional verification for account security.
