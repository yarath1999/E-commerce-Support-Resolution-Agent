# Sample E-commerce Support Policies

## Shipping & Tracking
- Orders ship in 1–2 business days.
- Standard shipping typically arrives in 3–5 business days.
- Tracking links are emailed after the order ships.

## Returns & Refunds
- Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery for unused items.
- Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days after the return is received.
- Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the item arrived damaged.

## Order Changes
- You can cancel or modify an order within 60 minutes of placing it.
- After that window, the order may already be in fulfillment.
